# Activité 6 — Quatrième AG : le PDC / 4th AG: Community Development Plan

> Phase 3 · Activité 2 · Étape 5 · Source : Part 1, §6

## Aperçu (FR)

La finalisation du PDC se fait lors de la 4ème AG. Le facilitateur explique le PDC, les critères d'éligibilité, les sauvegardes, et la liste des sous-projets éligibles/non éligibles. Tous les sous-projets ne sont pas admissibles, mais les autres sont documentés dans le PDC pour d'autres sources de financement. Quatre sujets présentés : contenu du PDC ; critères d'admissibilité ; mesures de sauvegardes ; liste des sous-projets éligibles/non éligibles.

## Quick reference (EN)

The main output of the planning phase is the **PDC (Community Development Plan)**, finalized at the **4th General Assembly** (whole community invited, quorum required). The facilitator presents four topics: **PDC content · eligibility criteria · safeguards · the list of eligible/ineligible sub-projects.** All sub-projects (fundable or not) are documented in the PDC so they can be considered for other funding.

- The PDC describes the village's current situation, development challenges, vision and proposed interventions. It covers **5 years**, is the product of the whole community, and must not exceed **50 pages**.
- **Eligibility:** the village allocation (33,5 M FCFA) guides how many sub-projects can be funded; the grant is per village (not per sub-project); total must not exceed the allocation; no sub-project may increase climate vulnerability; villages may collaborate on a shared sub-project; sub-projects come from the 3rd-AG priorities; ineligible ones are still documented.
- **Safeguards:** the project rests on the **CGES** framework; instruments include CGES, CPR, PGMO, PMPP and **environmental & social screening**. Sub-projects are classified A/B/C (Mali law) or high/substantial/moderate/low risk (World Bank). A/B → EIES + PGES (+ PAR if needed); C → NIES.
- The facilitator produces a single PDF of the PDC, prints ≥ 2 copies (one at the communal office, one at the village B-CDV), and shares the electronic file with FT, DCC specialists, ERAR and the regional branch.
