# Activité 2 — Première AG : orientation / 1st General Assembly: orientation

> Phase 1 · Activité 2 · Étape 2 · Source : Part 1, §2

## Quick reference (EN)

The first cycle step is the **1st General Assembly**, with two key goals: (1) present basic information on MARN and the PCRSS project; (2) introduce and plan the setting-up of the B-CDVFQ (the 2nd AG). About half a day.

**Before the meeting:** check attendance and quorum (50% households, 30% women); use the village loudspeaker to mobilize; brief the chief to say a few opening words; arrange the space so all can see; make project posters visible; ensure women are well placed (not at the back).

**Start of meeting:** clear intro of objectives; chief's words on community development; ground rules (equal, active participation; encourage women and vulnerable groups). Then the facilitator presents: brief intro to MARN; intro to PCRSS; the project cycle; the main actors and their functions. Hold a Q&A after each topic — the community asks the facilitator (not the reverse).

The facilitator introduces the project via **16 key points** (what/why/components/how it's implemented/gender/climate/cycle/community bodies/FC-FT roles/grant amount = **33,5 M FCFA per village**/sub-project selection/maintenance/E&S management/rights & responsibilities/MGP/avoiding unrealistic expectations). Then presents the **6-phase cycle**, the **main actors**, and organizes the **2nd AG** (election of the B-CDVFQ by secret ballot — strongly encourage women candidates).

## Tâches du facilitateur (checklist)

- [ ] Vérifier le quorum (50% ménages, 30% femmes) avant de démarrer.
- [ ] Faire ouvrir par le chef ; poser les règles de base.

- [ ] Planifier la 2ème AG et encourager les candidatures (surtout féminines).
