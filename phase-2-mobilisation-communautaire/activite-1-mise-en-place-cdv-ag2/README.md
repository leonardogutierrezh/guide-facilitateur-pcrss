# Activité 3 — Deuxième AG : mise en place du B-CDV / 2nd AG: setting up the CDV

> Phase 2 · Activité 1 · Étape 3 · Source : Part 1, §3

## Quick reference (EN)

The 2nd General Assembly sets up the **B-CDVFQ, the CC and the CGP by secret-ballot election**. The facilitator explains the CDV structure and bodies, the **code of conduct** (all elected members sign), and the selection rules, then runs the election **post by post** — first the B-CDVFQ, then the specialized committees (the bureau posts have stricter requirements and should be filled by the best people).

**Selection rules:** all members are volunteers (unpaid; training + allowances); high integrity; ≥ 18 years; ≥ 1 literate member per body; treasurer literate or helped by secretary; quorum 2/3; **≥ 30% women**; resignation → replacement by village vote; members removable for misconduct/poor performance; all sign the code of conduct.

**Election method:** secret ballot preferred for B-CDVFQ and CC; a post may be validated by consensus if there's a known traditional role and no objection (facilitators ensure no one is pressured). **CGP members are designated by their specific groups by consensus** (election only if no consensus). Ensure at least 2 more candidates than posts to avoid the embarrassment of a lone loser. Encourage women, youth and marginalized groups to stand, including for president.

**After election:** a capacity-building session for B-CDV, CGP and CC is held before the 3rd AG (see the B-CDV & Specialized Committees Training Manual).

## Tâches du facilitateur (checklist)

- [ ] Expliquer structure du CDV, code de conduite, règles de sélection.
- [ ] Installer le bureau de vote (isoloir, urnes couvertes) — scrutin secret.
- [ ] Élire poste par poste : B-CDV puis comités ; ≥ 2 candidats de plus que de postes.
- [ ] Respecter les quotas femmes ; CGP par consensus.
- [ ] Faire signer le code de conduite à chaque élu.
- [ ] Planifier la formation des comités avant la 3ème AG.
