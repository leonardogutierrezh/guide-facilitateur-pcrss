# Activité — Clôture des sous-projets / Sub-project closure (Step 12)

> Phase 6 · Activité 1 · Étape 12 · Source : Part 3, §2

## Quick reference (EN)

FC and FT play a role in closing sub-projects and must know the activities and forms. Some prepare the **social audit**.

A sub-project closes for **two reasons**: (1) **successful completion** → technical reception, lifting of reserves, provisional reception; or (2) **cessation** → contract suspension or termination if there's a problem.

**Successful completion** when: built per approved design/specs; funds used correctly; the **final inspection form (T15)** and **final financial forms (F19, F20)** are completed and submitted. Sequence: contractor announces completion → B-CDVFQ (with FT) informs the regional branch, Maire and SDE and sets the inspection day → site visit by B-CDVFQ, CTMO and FT → reserves issued if specs unmet → final inspection PV (T15) signed → within 15 days of the reserve-lifting PV, **provisional reception (T14)** → 7 days later, the **sub-project financial completion report (F19)** → submit T15 & F19 to the regional branch and Maire → AR checks funds vs approved budget → within one month, FC/FT facilitate the **social audit** and submit the results report (**OP5**) → FT/SC enter T15, F19, OP5 into the DCC app/SIG.

**Suspension/termination:** for force majeure (natural disaster, deteriorating security) or material breach. Corrective actions first (B-CDVFQ + FT, consult technical services & AR infrastructure specialist; amendment co-signed; SC monitors). If too serious/ineffective: suspend (T9), then terminate if the objective can't be met (T17); unspent funds returned to the project.

**Financial closure:** CTMO provides T15; treasurer (with FC/FT) prepares F19; F19 posted on the public notice board; after all sub-projects, treasurer prepares the **investment-cycle financial completion report (F20)**. Four copies each of F19 and F20 (3 paper + 1 digital): B-CDVFQ (malle), Maire, public notice board, regional branch (digital).

## Tâches du facilitateur (checklist)

- [ ] Organiser inspection finale (T15), levée des réserves, réception provisoire (T14).
- [ ] Préparer le rapport financier d'achèvement du sous-projet (F19) et du cycle (F20).
- [ ] Faciliter l'audit social (OP5) sous un mois.
- [ ] Saisir T15, F19, OP5 dans l'application DCC/SIG.
- [ ] En cas de problème : actions correctives, puis suspension (T9) / arrêt (T17) ; reverser les fonds non dépensés.
