# Activité — Audit social / Social audit (Step 13)

> Phase 6 · Activité 3 · Étape 13 · Source : Part 3, §5

## Quick reference (EN)

Every community that has done a sub-project holds a **social audit** — a half-day open village meeting at the end of each investment cycle (once the sub-project is complete) to review the process and results and make the B-CDVFQ accountable. It is crucial and must be carefully planned and supported by FC/FT.

**Objectives:** promote accountability; promote learning (what worked / didn't / needs improving); make recommendations for the next cycle; strengthen ownership; appreciate results. **Quorum:** at least half the households present, ideally equal men/women, special effort for women, vulnerable people and persons with disabilities. The chief, STDE, Maire, OSC/NGOs and local workers attend.

**Five components:** meeting preparation (date set, invitations ≥ 3 days ahead, presentations prepared); **site visit**; **presentations** by the B-CDVFQ bodies; **Community Satisfaction Card** (secret-ballot vote on Process and Results); **open group discussion & feedback** → agreed recommendations.

**Agenda (≈ 180 min):** opening (chief) → objectives/agenda (FC/FT) → B-CDVFQ activities (President) → implementation & results (CTMO) → financial management (Treasurer) → procurement (CEO) → operation & maintenance (CEM) → grievances (CGP) → participation (Organisation officers) → satisfaction card (30 min) → group discussions (30 min) → group presentations (20 min) → summary (FC/FT) → closing (chief).

After: minutes within 10 days, signed by the President, posted publicly in local languages within 15 days; copies to ERAR, regional branch and Maire within 15 days; results recorded in **OP5**. Communal results feed a **Multi-Stakeholder Review** at commune level.
