# Activité — Gestion financière communautaire / Community financial management

> Phase 5 · Activité 3 · Étape 11 · Source : Part 2, §8

## Quick reference (EN)

In DCC, the community receives the sub-project funds directly and must manage them. The **B-CDVFQ, especially the treasurer** (with FC and the regional accounting assistant), keeps the books, guards the funds and the bank account, prepares budgets, makes authorized payments, prepares records/reports, and returns any balance to the village account after completion.

**Grant prerequisites:** be formally created (récépissé); have statutes & internal rules; have an AG-approved community development plan; be on the project's village list; open a bank account under the **dual signature** of the CDVFQ President and Treasurer; sign a **tripartite agreement** (PCRSS, Commune, CDVFQ).

**Grant in 3 tranches:** **20%** at agreement signature (includes operating costs ≤ 3% of the grant), **50%** at signature of the main works contract + bank statement, **30%** once 75% of disbursed funds is justified. Eligible expenses: all sub-project costs (except negative-list projects); admin/procurement costs (**≤ 3%**). Any unused balance stays in the community for development activities (same approval process) — it cannot be distributed as cash or carried to next year's allocation.

**Payments:** dual-signature cheque or transfer (President + Treasurer); invoices stamped "PAYÉ" and filed; required supporting documents per expense. **Petty cash ceiling: 50,000 FCFA** (treasurer is sole holder; recorded in the cash book F13). Since 02 July 2021, normalized invoices with the provider's **NIF** are required. Records kept **10 years**. Forms **F1–F18**.

## Tâches du facilitateur (checklist)

- [ ] Appuyer l'ouverture du compte (double signature) et la convention tripartite (F1).
- [ ] Aider aux appels de fonds (20/50/30%) avec les pièces requises.
- [ ] Veiller aux paiements à double signature et aux pièces justificatives complètes.
- [ ] Tenir cahier de caisse (F13), cahier de banque (F15), fiche de suivi financier (F4).
- [ ] Respecter le plafond petite caisse (50 000) et les factures normalisées (NIF).
- [ ] Préparer le rapport technique et financier semestriel (F17).
