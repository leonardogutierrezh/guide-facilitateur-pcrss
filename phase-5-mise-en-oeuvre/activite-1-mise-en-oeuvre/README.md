# Activité — Mise en œuvre des sous-projets / Sub-project implementation (Steps 10–11)

> Phase 5 · Activité 1 · Étape "10–11" · Source : Part 2, §6

## Quick reference (EN)

After procurement, the community moves to implementation — Steps 10 and 11.

**Step 10 — set up & train CTMO and CEM:** train the CTMO to monitor works (technical, environmental, social, GBV/SH/SEA aspects); train the B-CDVFQ on financial management (analysing payment certificates and invoices, validity of performance bonds); set up and train the **CEM** (Operation & Maintenance Committee), preferably skilled workers in masonry, plumbing, electricity, carpentry.

**Step 11 — implementation monitoring:** monthly works supervision (to inform the project of progress); weekly site monitoring meetings (keep the site logbook up to date); **monthly General Assemblies** at payment times to inform the community of progress and financial transactions and get feedback; track cumulative spending (justify tranche 1) and request the **2nd tranche (50%)**. Distinct flows for projects with/without fiche (STDE involved for without-fiche).

The monthly AG is essential to DCC: the community must be informed of construction progress, financial transactions against the village allocation, complaints received, problems with the contractor, causes of delays, etc. Minimum one per month, more often if needed. **14 works-monitoring forms (T1–T14).**

## Tâches du facilitateur (checklist)

- [ ] Former le CTMO (suivi des travaux) et le B-CDV (gestion financière).
- [ ] Appuyer la mise en place et la formation du CEM.
- [ ] Tenir les réunions hebdomadaires de chantier et les AG mensuelles.
- [ ] Suivre les dépenses cumulées et préparer la demande de 2ème tranche.
