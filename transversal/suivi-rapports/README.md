# Activité 8 — Suivi et élaboration de rapports / Monitoring & reporting

> Transversal · Étape "8 (Part 1)" · Source : Part 1, §8

## Quick reference (EN)

Facilitators and supervisors monitor the work of the B-CDVFQ and committees to assess progress and results. All levels (region, commune, supervisors, FC/FT, B-CDV) produce regular reports and keep records well filed.

**Monitoring** = a system to collect information on implementation to guide management decisions — on activity progress, resource use, operational problems & solutions, and results. **Why:** activities finished on time and well; resources used correctly; problems identified and solved; stakeholders informed; lessons learned; accountability. **Who:** all stakeholders (UEP, MARN, AR, ERAR, DCC Expert, STDE, FC/FT, Supervisors, CDV, community); the B-CDV monitors its own activities. **How:** interview community and B-CDV members; inspect B-CDV records and actual construction; use monitoring forms.

There are monitoring/reporting levels: village, commune, region, national, and supranational (CRC). The **SIG/MIS** is the central management information system; forms must be entered into the **DCC and MGP apps** (and GEMS) on time and synchronized to the UEP server. Documentation must be retained and accessible for audit.

- [ ] Suivre les activités du B-CDV et des comités (interviews, inspection, formulaires).
- [ ] Saisir à temps dans les applications DCC/MGP et GEMS ; synchroniser.
- [ ] Produire les rapports mensuels et de fin de sous-projet aux échéances.
- [ ] Veiller au classement/archivage des documents.
